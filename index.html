Armenia was the first country in the world that adopted Christianityas a state religion. It happened in 301 AD with the assistance of Gregory the Illuminator and Armenian king Trdat III.
The country began the destruction of pagan temples and the construction of Christian churches.
The very first was the Echmiadzin Cathedral, which became the first Christian cathedral
in the world. You can see this magnificent creation in the town of Echmiadzin, the religious centre of all Armenians.
